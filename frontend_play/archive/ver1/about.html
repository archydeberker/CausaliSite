Causali

Why is Causali better than everything else?

All the other platforms for quantified self and personal science allow you measure things. That's cool, but it's only half the scientific method. The other half is to control things. With Causali, you get to conduct proper experiments, with randomised variables.

Why did you make Causali?

We're three science geeks (see below) and we'd like to use our (long, arduous, hard) training to improve our (and others') lives. We're enthusiastic self-experimenters, so we built a platform to make this easier.
